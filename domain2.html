Domain 2: Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Mitigations (22%)

This domain covers common threat actors, attack vectors, vulnerabilities, and mitigation strategies.

Key Topics

  • Threat Actors (Nation-State, Hacktivists, Organized Crime)
  • Attack Vectors (Phishing, Malware, Social Engineering)
  • Vulnerability Management
  • Indicators of Malicious Activity
  • Security Assessment Techniques

Study Notes

  • Phishing: Deceptive emails to steal information.
  • Ransomware: Encrypts data for ransom.
  • Vulnerability Scanning: Identifies system weaknesses.
  • Patch Management: Keeps systems up to date.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating people to gain access.
