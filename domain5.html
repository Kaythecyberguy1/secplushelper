Domain 5: Security Program Management and Oversight (20%)

This domain covers security awareness, policy development, assessment activities, third-party risk management, and business continuity.

Key Topics

  • Security Awareness Practices
  • Policies, Plans, and Procedures
  • Security Assessment Activities
  • Third-Party Risk Management
  • Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

Study Notes

  • Security Awareness: Training users to recognize and respond to threats.
  • Policy Development: Creating and maintaining security policies and procedures.
  • Risk Management: Assessing and managing risks from third parties.
  • Business Continuity: Ensuring operations continue during and after incidents.
